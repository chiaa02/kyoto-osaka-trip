Exploring Kyoto & Osaka

In July 2025, I traveled to Kyoto and Osaka with my friends for a short summer getaway. During our trip, we explored famous temples and shrines in Kyoto, walked through the beautiful streets of Gion, and enjoyed the peaceful atmosphere of traditional Japanese culture. We also visited the iconic Fushimi Inari Shrine and admired its thousands of bright red torii gates.

After Kyoto, we headed to Osaka, where we experienced the city's lively atmosphere. We visited Dotonbori, tried delicious local street food, explored Osaka Castle, and spent time shopping in Shinsaibashi. The trip was a perfect mix of history, culture, entertainment, and unforgettable memories with friends. It was one of the most enjoyable trips I have had since moving to Japan.
